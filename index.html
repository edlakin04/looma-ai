Onboarding flow
Step 1 of 5
Step 1

Deposit a minimum of £500 into the broker

Start by funding your broker account with at least £500. This gives the Looma AI bot enough capital to manage trades effectively and spread risk across positions.

Note: Your funds always remain in your own broker account — Looma AI does not hold or custody your money at any point.

Step 2

Download the MetaTrader platform

Install the MetaTrader platform (MT4 or MT5, depending on the broker) on your phone, tablet, or computer. This is where you can watch live trades and your balance in real-time.

Tip: Once installed, log in using the broker details you receive. You’ll instantly see your account, open trades, and full trade history.

Step 3

Connect your account to the Looma AI bot

Our team helps you connect your MetaTrader account to the Looma AI bot. Once connected, the bot begins placing trades on your behalf using our strategy and risk parameters.

Important: Your funds stay in your own broker account at all times. Looma AI simply has permission to place trades — you keep full control over deposits and withdrawals.

Step 4

Watch earnings & choose withdrawals

As trades run, you can watch your balance and equity move in real-time inside MetaTrader. You decide when to withdraw profits, top up your balance, or let it compound.

Risk reminder: Trading is never guaranteed and losses can occur. Only use money you can afford to lose and adjust your withdrawal and compounding choices to match your own risk tolerance.

Step 5

Join the WhatsApp group & start today

Join the Looma AI WhatsApp group. This is where we guide you through the set up, see updates, and get help at any stage — before, during, or after your account is connected.

Good to know: There are no dashboards or logins to remember. Everything from onboarding to questions runs through WhatsApp and your broker/MetaTrader app.
